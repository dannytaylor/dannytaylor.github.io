about the crew

Velocity/Murda Dovez, is a top tier eSports collective of friends. The group has had great sucess in Heroes of Newerth, CS:S, CS:GO, Monaco, Dota 2, and TF2 despite not having any listable accomplishments. Our crowning achievement is our legacy in City of Heroes. Undesputably the best team ever to grace the arena on the Test Server, we dominated the PvP scene for longer and more convincingly than any other. While it's been predicted that we shall once again dominate in the upcoming City of Titans, our place in history has already been established in Paragon City.
