about us

Velocity/Murda Dovez, is a top tier eSports collective of friends. With the dawn of the internet and the digital age (also known as the Age of Socom) Velocity began to take on the world in competitive eSports. Our 1-man branch in Socom was arguably in the top 3 most feared groups out of Sacramento. Our Monaco heist team is undeafeated. More recently the group has had great sucess in Heroes of Newerth, CS:S, CS:GO, Monaco, Dota 2, and TF2 despite not having any listable accomplishments.

Our crowning achievement though, is our legacy in City of Heroes. Undesputably the best team ever to grace the arena on the Test Server, we dominated the PvP scene for longer and more convincingly than any other. While it's been predicted that we shall once again dominate in the upcoming City of Titans, our place in history has already been established in Paragon City.
